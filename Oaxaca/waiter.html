Staff Portal

This page is for staff access only. This page is designed for our team members to streamline operations and enhance communication. Here, you'll find essential tools and information to help you manage orders efficiently and stay informed about delivery times.

You are able to do the following functions on this page
  • Cancel and manage orders
  • View and change order status
  • Confirm deliveries
  • View delivery times


Your privacy and the security of sensitive information are our top priorities. Access is limited to authorized personnel only. Please ensure that you keep your login credentials confidential. If you have any issues, please raise it to the IT Staff.
